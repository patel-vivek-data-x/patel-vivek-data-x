### 📂 Projects

#### [FIFA 23 Talent Distribution Analysis](https://github.com/patel-vivek-data-x)
> Descriptive analytics · Python · DuckDB · SQL · Pandas · Matplotlib · Excel

End-to-end analysis of how FIFA 23 player ratings distribute across leagues, clubs, and positions. Built with a SQL-first pipeline, pre-committed cross-validation strategies, and explicit governance boundaries to prevent overclaiming on game-derived data.

**Key findings:**
- Identified two distinct league archetypes — depth-based vs. elite-concentration
- Female ratings exceed male ratings at all 15 positions by 7–14 points, confirmed as a scale calibration difference, not a quality comparison
- 97.31% female league-attribution gap formally documented as a structural data constraint
